Concussion Diagnostic Test

This test will help determine whether you have a concussion. Although it does not replace seeing a physician, it can assess signs on brain injury. If you are experiencing or have experienced losing consciousness, inability to awaken, seizures, numbness, or confusion at any point after your injury, call 911 or go to your closest emergency room immediately.

DISCLAIMER: DO NOT USE THIS APP TO DIAGNOSE A CONCUSSION

How To Use:

  • Best to be evaluated by another person: Ask the questions to the patient and log answers appropriately
  • If answering for yourself, answer truthfully to the best of your ability
